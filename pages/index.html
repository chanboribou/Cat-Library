About Us

Cat Library is an online library that explicitly offers the epub and pdf version of the Famous Russian Literature Author, Fyodor Dostoevsky and the Famous American Literature Author, Jane Austen. We first opened this website back in the pandemic when people couldn't buy physical books due to the infection and social distancing which made it harder for people to buy books and led to economic downfall. We also understand the security concerns of piracy since some people have downloaded books or other stuffs illegally on the internet which is why we will link the sources of where we get the epub or pdf version to avoid getting copyright infringement and we will not sell the copy of them either since it’s not our original work.
